Bibliography

Timothy Hallinan

Timothy Hallinan is an author of several thriller and mystery series. Part of his website is dedicated to helping novelists finish their troublesome stories, and it's chock-full of useful advice for all kinds of storytellers.

Limyaael's Rants

Limyaael is a fantasy author who wrote over 400 articles outlining what NOT to do in writing, which were compiled by the good folks at Curiosity Quills. While the advice naturally skews towards the fantasy genre, Limyaael has a good head on her shoulders and much of her advice applies to any type of storytelling.

Just Write

Created by Sage Hyden, this channel dissects movies and TV shows and figures out what makes them tick (or not). Mr. Hyden is especially talented at pinpointing the emotional core of a scene, explaining how and why certain pieces stick with us and others fall flat.

Optimize Literally Everything

While best known for his research on artificial intelligence, Eliezer Yudkowsky is also a gifted writer and staunch proponent of characters who make reasonable decisions. This series of articles outlines the process for creating intelligent characters.
