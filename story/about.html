About

My name is Sam Frost and I'm a self-taught programmer with a passion for storytelling.

The market for resources on writing is, let's say, inundated. If you want to learn how to do any kind of writing there are countless sites that offer advice and they usually look prettier than mine.

But I have a problem with the way the internet tends to talk about storytelling. Too often people decide that their way is the way of storytelling.
